Model: Claude 3 Haiku
Provider: Anthropic
Release: March 2024

Claude 3 Haiku is Anthropic's fastest and most cost-effective model, optimized for quick responses and high-volume applications while maintaining good quality and safety standards.

Key Capabilities:
- Very fast response times
- Cost-effective processing
- Good basic reasoning
- Reliable text generation
- Safe and helpful responses
- Efficient resource usage

Technical Specifications:
- Context window: 200k tokens
- Training cutoff: April 2024
- Response speed: Very fast (~200ms)
- Cost: $0.25/1M input tokens, $1.25/1M output tokens

Best Use Cases:
- High-volume applications
- Real-time chat and customer service
- Quick content generation
- Simple analysis and summarization
- Cost-sensitive applications
- Fast prototyping and development

Limitations:
- Less sophisticated reasoning than larger models
- May struggle with complex tasks
- Limited creative capabilities
- Basic performance on specialized domains
